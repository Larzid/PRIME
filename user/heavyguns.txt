@FHeavy guns @E(Strength)@H

Big and heavy cannons are different from most guns you aim with both hands.
These have great recoil and often are very cumbersome.  This makes your
@EStrength@H help with aiming them instead of @EDexterity@H as one might assume.

Examples of such guns:
 * pulse rifle
 * mining laser
 * railgun
 * plasma cannon
 * auto-cannon
