@FSearch @E(Intelligence)@H

Gives you a better chance to find secrets when you are actively searching for
them.  To make an attempt stand still in an area where you think hidden things
are located.  All adjacent spaces will be checked.  You may need to do this
a few times to see some results.

In general this is a non-crucial skill.  It is recommended to spend only a few
of your precious skill points on this.
